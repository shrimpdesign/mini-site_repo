Title : Lover Bot
Material: Acrylic Yarn, Fiberfill, Thin Wire
Size: 4 in x 4 in x 7 in

"Lover Bot" is an endearing crochet robot designed to embody the spirit of love and companionship. Crafted from soft acrylic yarn, this charming figure features intricate details that highlight its robotic yet affectionate nature while holding a daisy in their left hand. The artist has skillfully incorporated thin wire into the design, allowing for subtle posability while maintaining the robot's structural integrity. Filled with plush fiberfill, "Lover Bot" stands at 7 inches tall and measures 4 inches in both width and depth, making it a perfect collectible for crochet enthusiasts or a heartfelt gift for a loved one. This handcrafted crochet creation is sure to bring warmth and joy to anyone who encounters it.
